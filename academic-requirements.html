Degree Completion Requirements

Our rigorous academic standards ensure that every graduate of Freedom College of Excellence is prepared for a lifetime of leadership and purposeful contribution.

Baccalaureate Studies

Undergraduate Degree Requirements

  • check_circle

    Total Credits

    Completion of at least 120 semester hours of approved coursework.

  • check_circle

    Grade Point Average

    A minimum cumulative GPA of 2.0 on a 4.0 scale.

  • check_circle

    Residency Requirement

    Minimum of 30 credits completed directly at FCoE.

Core Curriculum

All undergraduate students participate in our signature "Excellence Core," a series of interdisciplinary courses focused on critical thinking, ethics, and global citizenship.
