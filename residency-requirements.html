Residency Requirements

Minimum Credits at Freedom College

Purpose

The Residency Requirement ensures that students complete a substantial portion of their degree at Freedom College of Excellence to guarantee the quality and integrity of awarded degrees.

Definition of Residency

Residency refers to credit hours earned through coursework completed at Freedom College, including:

  • On-campus courses;
  • Online courses offered by Freedom College;
  • Hybrid courses;
  • Study abroad programs sponsored by Freedom College;
  • Consortium courses pre-approved and coordinated by Freedom College.

Not Counted Toward Residency: Transfer credits from other institutions, CLEP exams, AP credits, IB credits, or military training credits.

Residency Requirements by Degree Level

Degree Level Total Credits Minimum Residency Percentage
Associate Degree 60 credits 15 credits 25%
Bachelor's Degree 120 credits 30 credits 25%
Master's Degree 30-36 credits 23-27 credits 75%

Major-Specific Residency

In addition to overall residency requirements, undergraduate students must complete a minimum number of credits in their major at Freedom College:

  • Bachelor's Degree: Minimum 15 credits in the major at Freedom College;
  • Associate Degree: Minimum 9 credits in the major/program at Freedom College.

This ensures students receive instruction in their primary field of study from Freedom College faculty.

Minor Residency

Students declaring a minor must complete a minimum of 9 credits of the minor at Freedom College.

Second Degree Residency

Students pursuing a second bachelor's or associate degree must complete an additional:< /p>

  • Second Bachelor's: Minimum 30 new credits at Freedom College beyond the first degree, including at least 15 credits in the new major;
  • Second Associate: Minimum 15 new credits at Freedom College beyond the first degree.

Graduate Degree Residency

Master's degree students must complete at least 75% of their program at Freedom College. This typically means:

  • 30-credit programs: Minimum 23 credits at Freedom College;
  • 36-credit programs: Minimum 27 credits at Freedom College.

No more than 25% of credits may be transferred from other institutions.

Consortium and Study Abroad Courses

Courses taken through consortium agreements or study abroad programs sponsored and coordinated by Freedom College count toward residency requirements. Students must:

  • Obtain pre-approval from their academic advisor and the Registrar's Office;
  • Enroll through Freedom College's consortium or study abroad office;
  • Pay tuition to Freedom College (not the partner institution).

Online and Hybrid Courses

Online and hybrid courses offered by Freedom College count fully toward residency requirements, as they are taught by Freedom College faculty and adhere to the same academic standards as on-campus courses.

Exceptions and Waivers

In rare circumstances, students may petition for a waiver or modification of residency requirements. Petitions must be submitted to the Provost and include:

  • Written justification for the waiver request;
  • Documentation of extenuating circumstances (e.g., military deployment, medical emergency);
  • Recommendation from the academic advisor and department chair.

Waivers are granted on a case-by-case basis and are not guaranteed.

Verification of Residency

The Registrar's Office verifies that students meet residency requirements before conferring degrees. Students can view their residency status through the degree audit in the student portal.
