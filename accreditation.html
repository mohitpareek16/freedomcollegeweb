What Does Accreditation Mean?

Accreditation is a voluntary, peer-review process that assures the quality and integrity of an institution's educational programs. Regional accreditation by HLC means that Freedom College:

  • Meets rigorous standards for educational quality;
  • Engages in ongoing assessment and improvement;
  • Operates with integrity and transparency;
  • Provides adequate resources to support student success;
  • Maintains qualified faculty and staff.

Benefits of Attending an Accredited Institution

Students attending Freedom College benefit from HLC accreditation through:

  • Federal Financial Aid Eligibility: Only students at accredited institutions are eligible for federal student loans and grants;
  • Transfer Credit Acceptance: Credits from accredited institutions are more readily accepted by other colleges;
  • Graduate School Admission: Most graduate programs require applicants to hold degrees from accredited institutions;
  • Employer Recognition: Employers value degrees from accredited institutions;
  • Professional Licensure: Many professions require degrees from accredited programs for licensure.

Programmatic Accreditation

In addition to institutional accreditation, several academic programs at Freedom College hold specialized accreditation from discipline-specific agencies:

Business Programs

Accreditation Council for Business Schools and Programs (ACBSP)
Our Bachelor of Business Administration and Master of Business Administration programs are accredited by ACBSP.
Website: www.acbsp.org

Education Programs

Council for the Accreditation of Educator Preparation (CAEP)
Our Teacher Education programs are accredited by CAEP, ensuring graduates meet national standards for teaching excellence.
Website: www.caepnet.org

Nursing Programs

Commission on Collegiate Nursing Education (CCNE)
Our Bachelor of Science in Nursing (BSN) program is accredited by CCNE.
Website: www.aacnnursing.org/ccne

Engineering Programs

ABET (Engineering Accreditation Commission)
Our Engineering programs are accredited by ABET, the global accreditor of college programs in applied science, computing, engineering, and engineering technology.
Website: www.abet.org

State Authorization

Freedom College is authorized to operate by the [State] Higher Education Coordinating Board and complies with all state regulations for degree-granting institutions.

Authorization allows the College to:

  • Award degrees and certificates;
  • Operate physical campus locations within the state;
  • Enroll out-of-state students in online programs through the National Council for State Authorization Reciprocity Agreements (NC-SARA).

Public Disclosure

As required by the Higher Learning Commission, Freedom College publicly discloses information about its accreditation status, academic programs, student outcomes, and financial operations. This information is available on our website and through the Registrar's Office.

Student Right to Information

Students have the right to:

  • Review accreditation reports and action letters;
  • Submit complaints to accrediting agencies if they believe the College is not in compliance with accreditation standards;
  • Access information about program-specific accreditation relevant to their major.
