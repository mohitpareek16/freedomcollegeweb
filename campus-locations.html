Campus Visits

Prospective students are encouraged to visit our campuses. We offer:

  • Guided Campus Tours: Monday-Friday at 10:00 AM and 2:00 PM; Saturdays at 11:00 AM (Main Campus only)
  • Information Sessions: Learn about admissions, financial aid, and academic programs
  • Meet with Faculty: Schedule appointments to discuss specific programs
  • Virtual Tours: Available online at www.freedomcollege.edu/visit

Schedule a Visit:

  • Metered street parking
