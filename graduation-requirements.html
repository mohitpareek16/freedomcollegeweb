Graduation Requirements

Earning Your Degree

Purpose

This policy outlines the requirements students must fulfill to earn a degree from Freedom College of Excellence.

General Graduation Requirements

To be eligible for graduation, students must:

  • Complete all degree requirements as specified in the academic catalog;
  • Achieve the minimum cumulative GPA for their program;
  • Complete the residency requirement;
  • Settle all financial obligations to the College;
  • Submit an Application for Graduation by the published deadline.

Degree Requirements by Program Level

Associate Degree

  • Total Credits: 60 semester hours
  • Minimum GPA: 2.0
  • Residency: Minimum 15 credits completed at Freedom College
  • General Education: 18-24 credits in core curriculum areas

Bachelor's Degree

  • Total Credits: 120 semester hours
  • Minimum GPA: 2.0 overall; 2.5 in major
  • Residency: Minimum 30 credits completed at Freedom College, including at least 15 credits in the major
  • General Education: 36-42 credits in core curriculum areas
  • Major: 30-48 credits in primary field of study
  • Upper-Level Coursework: Minimum 45 credits at the 300-400 level

Master's Degree

  • Total Credits: 30-36 semester hours (varies by program)
  • Minimum GPA: 3.0
  • Residency: Minimum 75% of credits completed at Freedom College
  • Capstone Requirement: Thesis, comprehensive exam, or capstone project (program-specific)
  • Time Limit: All requirements must be completed within 5 years of initial enrollment

General Education Core Curriculum

All undergraduate degree programs include general education requirements in the following areas:

  • Communication: English Composition, Speech (6-9 credits)
  • Mathematics: College-level mathematics or quantitative reasoning (3-6 credits)
  • Natural Sciences: Biology, Chemistry, Physics, or Environmental Science (6-8 credits, including lab)
  • Social & Behavioral Sciences: Psychology, Sociology, Economics, Political Science (6-9 credits)
  • Humanities & Fine Arts: Literature, Philosophy, History, Art, Music (6-9 credits)
  • Global & Cultural Awareness: Foreign language, cultural studies, diversity courses (3-6 credits)

Application for Graduation

Students must submit an Application for Graduation by the published deadline:

  • Fall Graduation: Application due by September 15
  • Spring Graduation: Application due by January 15
  • Summer Graduation: Application due by May 1

Graduation Fee: $100 (non-refundable)

Degree Audit

Students are encouraged to review their degree audit regularly via the student portal. The degree audit tracks:

  • Completed requirements;
  • In-progress courses;
  • Outstanding requirements.

Students should meet with their academic advisor at least once per year to review progress toward degree completion.

Commencement Participation

Students who have completed all degree requirements or are within 6 credits of completion may participate in the Commencement ceremony. Students completing requirements in the summer following the ceremony will receive their diploma upon final completion.

Second Degree

Students who have already earned a degree from Freedom College or another institution may pursue a second degree in a different major. Requirements include:

  • Completion of all major requirements for the new degree;
  • Minimum 30 additional credits beyond the first degree;
  • Minimum residency of 15 credits at Freedom College;
  • Minimum GPA of 2.0 (undergraduate) or 3.0 (graduate).

Posthumous Degrees

In the event of a student's death, the College may award a posthumous degree if the student was in good academic standing and had completed at least 75% of degree requirements.

Catalog Year

Students follow the degree requirements in effect at the time of their initial enrollment (catalog year), provided they maintain continuous enrollment. Students who are absent for more than two consecutive semesters must follow the catalog in effect upon readmission.
